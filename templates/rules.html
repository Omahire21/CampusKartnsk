Community Rules

Let's keep CampusKart a trusted and respectful marketplace for all students.

1. Be Honest and Transparent

Accurately describe your items, including any flaws, wear and tear, or missing parts. Use genuine photos of the actual product, not stock images from the internet.

2. No Spam or Irrelevant Listings

Do not post duplicate listings for the same item. Only list items that are relevant to college students (textbooks, electronics, dorm essentials, etc.). Illegal items or services are strictly prohibited.

3. Respectful Communication

Treat fellow students with respect. Abusive language, harassment, or threats will result in an immediate and permanent ban from the platform.
