Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, list a product, or communicate with other users. This includes your name, college email, college name, and any messages sent through our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your info to provide and improve our services, verify student status, facilitate communication between buyers and sellers, and send you important security alerts.

3. Information Sharing

Your college name and username are visible to other logged-in students. We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers. We may disclose info if required by law or to protect our community's safety.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
