Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. Please read these guidelines before meeting anyone.

Meet in Public, Safe Locations

Always meet during daylight hours in busy, public areas of the campus such as the library, cafeteria, or student union. Never go to a stranger's dorm room or invite them to yours.

Verify the Item Before Paying

Inspect the product thoroughly to ensure it matches the description. For electronics, turn them on and check basic functionality before handing over any money.

Keep Conversations on CampusKart

Avoid sharing personal phone numbers or social media handles immediately. Use the in-app chat to discuss prices and meeting spots to maintain a record of your conversation in case of disputes.
